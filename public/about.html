About PastPocket

PastPocket is a unique platform that helps you create, manage, and preserve time capsules. With PastPocket, you can store memories, reminders, and significant milestones tied to locations.

Our system integrates location-based services to ensure that your time capsules are linked to meaningful places. We prioritize data security and ease of access so your memories are safe, well-organized, and can be relived anytime.

Join us in making memory preservation more meaningful!
